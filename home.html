Green. Red. The verdict.

Each goal is a column. Each day is a square. Did it — green. Didn't — red. Still open today — orange. No interpretation, no encouragement. Just the record, week after week.

On your home screen. Always honest.

The grid lives in a home-screen widget. Tap a square to mark it done — no need to open the app. The first thing you see when you pick up your phone is the truth about your week.

Every evening, the reckoning.

A midday nudge. An evening reckoning at 22:30. Reckon names exactly what you didn't do — by name. It acknowledges a clean day, too. It just won't be warm about it.

Built your way.

Five palettes built in. Edit any of them, or make your own from scratch. Light and dark, fully tuned. The only thing Reckon lets you decorate is how the truth looks.
